Gilbert Grape, Pt. 1; Selfishness at Home

I have a really close friend who is against modern, organized religion. (hey Conor!) This letter is a comment on his point of view. I understand his criticism.

Organized religion is defined as a group of people who follow a certain belief. Oftentimes, they believe that their group understands things a little more clearly than the other groups, otherwise there wouldn't be a need for another group.

Criticisms of organized religion

Organized religion better have a reason why they are different.

America has a heard mentality problem. We have a problem with doing things just because "everyone else is doing it." If everyone is voting for Joe Biden, then people will start to vote for him not because he is the better choice, but because everyone else is.

What I'm getting at is that, organized or not, you belong to a religion. America has its own religion. It's called clergy are celebrities. I am not the first person to make this observation. Government law is really not much different than religious law. What does religion use scriptures for? to teach lessons. What do movies and TV shows do? teach messages.
